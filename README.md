# foreign-tourists-poland-dashboard

## Project overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard analysing foreign tourist arrivals and expenditures in Poland between 2019 and 2024.  
The dashboard shows:

-Total number of tourists per year

-Expenditures by year

-YoY changes and CAGR

-Seasonal trends (tourist arrivals by month)

## Technologies and skills
  -Power BI
  
  -DAX measures
  
  -Power Query transformations
  
  -Data visualization & analysis

## Source
Central Statistical Office (CSO) – Border traffic and expences made by foreigners in Poland and Poles abroad (Ruch graniczny oraz wydatki cudzoziemców w Polsce i Polaków za granicą)

https://stat.gov.pl/obszary-tematyczne/ceny-handel/handel/ruch-graniczny-oraz-wydatki-cudzoziemcow-w-polsce-i-polakow-za-granica-w-2024-r-,15,11.html

The data is based on the survey “Travel of non-residents to Poland. Vehicle and passenger traffic at the Polish border with European Union countries”, conducted by Statistics Poland (GUS).

The survey provides estimates of both border traffic volumes and expenditures of visitors entering Poland.
